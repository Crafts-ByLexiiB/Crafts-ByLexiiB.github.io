Korean-Style In-N-Out Loaded Animal Fries

If you're a fan of In-N-Out's animal fries, get ready to meet your new obsession. This Korean-inspired twist layers crispy fries with melty American cheese, tender bulgogi beef, sweet caramelized onions, and a creamy, spicy kimchi gochujang sauce. The flavors and textures all come together in the most beautiful way and you'll understand why the fusion just makes perfect sense after the first bite.

Prep Time: 30 minutes Cook Time: 30 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour Yields: 4 to 5

Ingredients

IN-N-OUT SAUCE
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup kimchi, finely chopped
  • 2-3 tablespoons gochujang
  • 1 teaspoon granulated white sugar
    BULGOGI BEEF
